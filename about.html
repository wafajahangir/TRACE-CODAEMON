About Trace

We're revolutionizing NGO financial management through transparency, accountability, and technology

Our Mission

To create a transparent ecosystem where NGOs can showcase their impact and donors can support causes with complete confidence in how their contributions are utilized.

Our Vision

A world where every donation is traceable, every impact is measurable, and every NGO operates with full transparency and accountability.

Our Values

Transparency, accountability, trust, and impact. We believe in empowering both NGOs and donors through technology and data-driven insights.

Our Story

Trace was founded with a simple yet powerful idea: what if donors could see exactly where their money goes and what impact it creates? What if NGOs could prove their effectiveness and build trust through transparent reporting?

We built a platform that connects NGOs and donors through a unique point-based ranking system. NGOs earn points by documenting their work, submitting proof of impact, and demonstrating effective use of donations. This creates a competitive yet collaborative environment that drives excellence in the nonprofit sector.

Today, Trace serves hundreds of NGOs and thousands of donors, facilitating millions in transparent donations and creating real, measurable impact in communities around the world.
